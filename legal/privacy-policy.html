Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 19, 2026

Summary

QRGenPro is designed to collect as little as possible. The QR code generator itself runs entirely client-side: any text, URL, contact detail, or Wi-Fi password you enter is processed in your browser's memory to draw the code and is never transmitted to our servers, logged, or stored by us.

Information we don't collect

We do not require an account, do not ask for your email to use the generator, and do not store the content you encode into a QR code. Uploaded logo images are processed locally in your browser and are not uploaded anywhere.

Information collected automatically

Like most websites, our hosting provider (Cloudflare Pages) and any analytics we enable may collect standard technical data such as IP address, browser type, device type, and pages visited, for security and aggregate usage statistics. This data is not tied to the content you generate in the QR tool.

Cookies

QRGenPro does not use cookies for the QR generator itself. If advertising (e.g. Google AdSense) is enabled on this site, third-party cookies may be set by the ad provider to serve relevant ads; see "Advertising" below.

Advertising

This site may display ads served by third parties such as Google AdSense. These providers may use cookies or device identifiers to serve ads based on your visits to this and other sites. You can opt out of personalized advertising through Google's Ad Settings or the Digital Advertising Alliance opt-out page.

Third-party links

Content you generate (such as a URL QR code) may point to third-party sites. We aren't responsible for the privacy practices of destinations you choose to encode.

Children's privacy

This site is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent via the contact page.
