Accuracy of generated codes

QRGenPro generates QR codes based on standard QR encoding. While we aim for high reliability, scanning success can vary by device, camera quality, print resolution, lighting, and the amount of content encoded. Always test a code with more than one device before mass-printing it.

Not responsible for destination content

When you generate a QR code pointing to a URL, email address, or other destination, QRGenPro has no control over and is not responsible for the content, availability, or safety of that destination.

Wi-Fi and contact codes

Wi-Fi QR codes embed your network password directly in the image. Treat printed Wi-Fi and contact QR codes with the same care you'd give the underlying credentials — anyone who scans them gains that information.
